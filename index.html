FAQ's about Our Dental Clinic

1. What dental services do you provide? We offer general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, dental implants, root canal treatment, teeth whitening, and more.

2. How often should I visit the dentist? It is recommended to visit the dentist every 6 months for a regular check-up and cleaning.

3. Do you provide emergency dental care? Yes, we provide emergency dental care for issues like toothaches, broken teeth, or sudden dental pain. Please call us immediately in case of an emergency.

4. Is teeth whitening safe? Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by a qualified dentist. It helps brighten your smile without damaging your teeth.

5. Do you accept insurance or offer payment plans? Yes, we accept most dental insurance plans and also provide flexible payment options to make treatments affordable.

What should I do if I have a toothache?

Answer: If you have a toothache, rinse your mouth with warm water and gently floss to remove any food particles. Avoid placing painkillers directly on the gum. Contact your dentist immediately for proper treatment.

How long does a dental implant procedure take?

Answer: The procedure usually takes 1–2 hours, depending on the complexity. Complete healing and crown placement may take a few months.

Do kids also need regular dental check-ups?

Answer: Yes, children should visit the dentist every 6 months to ensure proper oral health, cavity prevention, and to guide them in developing good dental hygiene habits.
